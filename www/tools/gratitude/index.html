Gratitude

Description: Research shows that gratitude is essential for leading a happy life. This tool can help you increase feelings of gratitude.

Gratitude is a decision to see the good things in life as gifts, and a decision to have an attitude of thanks. It is the opposite of seeing good things as owed to us, or taking good things for granted. An attitude of gratefulness is the most important thing we can do to increase happiness. Therefore, it is important to learn ways to be grateful.

Research shows that the positive effects of gratitude are multiplied as you increase the following:
  • The strength of your sense of gratitude
  • How often you feel grateful
  • The number of things you are grateful for
  • The number of people you are grateful for

Try this technique:

Gratitude Letter

Think of a person you are grateful for. It could be someone who did something important for you, or a person you are thankful to simply have in your life. Take a few minutes to write a letter expressing your gratitude for this person. Write about the importance of their relationship with you. Try to be as specific as possible about the good things they have done for you or the specific ways they have positively affected your life. It's your decision if you actually want to deliver the letter. The most important thing is not that the person gets the letter, but that you take on a grateful attitude toward life. You can do this technique whenever you feel unhappy, and whenever you want to express your gratitude toward the important people in your life.
