Decide What to Do Now

The final step is to Decide what to do now. If you feel stressed, you can decrease the trigger's power by deciding to stay in the moment. You will find that you are able to handle the trigger after all. Your stress will go down the longer you stay in the moment. But, if your trigger made you very angry, your best bet is to take a time-out and leave the moment (see the Time Out tool for help). If you are in danger of hurting yourself for others, seek support right away, don't take any chances!
