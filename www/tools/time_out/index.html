Time Out

Description

This is a great tool when you just need a "break" from life's problems. It combines a relaxing breathing exercise with helpful tips to change your perspective.

Sometimes the most helpful thing is to take a "time out." This is helpful if your emotions are getting out of control, and especially helpful if your emotions might lead you to harm yourself or other people. The goal is avoid making a hard situation worse, and avoid doing something you'll later regret. Take a "time out" now by pressing the start button.
