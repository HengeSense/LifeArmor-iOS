Military Sexual Trauma

"Military Sexual Trauma" (MST) refers to sexual assault or harassment involving a female or male service member while in the military. Sexual assault, intentional sexual contact without consent, and sexual harassment, (verbal or physical harassment of a sexual nature), are traumatic events. This module provides information about MST, assesses MST symptoms, and offers activities to assist in addressing problems related to MST.
