Post-Traumatic Stress

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) is an anxiety disorder that can result after a person experiences a traumatic event or a series of traumatic events. Symptoms include increased arousal such as irritability and trouble sleeping, re-experiencing such as nightmares and flashbacks, and avoidance and numbing such as isolating from others. This module helps you identify what triggers your anxiety, find out about PTSD, assess your PTSD symptoms, learn tools to improve your symptoms and link to videos.
