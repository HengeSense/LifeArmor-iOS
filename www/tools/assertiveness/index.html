Assertiveness

Description: The goal of this tool is to help you learn to feel more control over situations in your life. Changing the words we use to think and speak about ourselves is a key way to feel more powerful.

The way we feel about ourselves is affected by the way we speak. Sometimes we use language that makes us feel out of control and powerless. When events seem to go against our wishes, it's important to use language that helps us feel powerful. One method is to see our interactions with others as a series of invitations. We can either accept or decline these invitations. When you view events as a series of invitations you will begin to feel more control over your life.

Here is how it sounds when you use the language of invitation:

Powerless Statement Reframed as an Invitation
She really made me mad when she said that She invited me to get angry by saying that
I'm really getting tired of being pushed around I'm regularly being invited to do things I don't want to do
I'm constantly being sucked into these arguments I'm constantly being invited to argue with him/her/them
He's always tugging at me to do what he wants He's always inviting me to follow his lead
She's trying to keep me from having fun She's inviting me to walk away from things I enjoy
