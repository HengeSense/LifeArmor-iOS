R.I.D.
Relax

Description

"Relax, Identify the Trigger, Decide what to Do" (R.I.D.) is a method for coping with strong emotions. It is good for reducing anger, anxiety, panic, and other emotional reactions. Use this tool to guide you in using R.I.D.

Take 30 seconds to Relax by breathing in and out slowly. As you breathe in think the word "let" and, as you breathe out, think "go."
